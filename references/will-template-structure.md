## Clauses

### 1. Revocation
"I [Full Name] date of birth [DOB] of [Full Address] do hereby revoke all former Wills and testamentary dispositions so far as they relate to my property of every kind wherever situate excluding that in [COUNTRY if applicable] and declare that the law of England & Wales shall apply to this my will hereinafter referred to as my Will in relation to my property of every kind wherever situate excluding that in [COUNTRY if applicable]"

### 2. Appointment of Executors
"I appoint [Primary Executor Name] to be the sole executor of this my Will but if my [relationship] is unable or unwilling to prove my Will then I APPOINT [Substitute Executor 1 Name] of [Address] and [Substitute Executor 2 Name] of [Address] to be the executors of this my Will (hereinafter referred to as 'my Executors')"

Trustee paragraph (always included):
"Always provided that if a trust is created in the following clauses of this my Will and no appointment of a trustee is made in relation to that trust I direct that my Executor shall be appointed as my trustee hereinafter referred to as 'my trustees' which expression shall include the trustee or trustees for the time being hereof"

### 3. Definition and Administration of my Estate
a) "In this my Will where the context so admits my Estate shall mean:"
  i) "all my real and personal property of every kind wherever situate excluding that in [COUNTRY] including that over which I have a general power of appointment and"
  ii) "the money investments and property from time to time representing all such property"
b) "My Executors shall hold my Estate upon trust"
  i) "to pay and discharge all my debts funeral testamentary and administration expenses and"
  ii) "to give effect to all legacies"

### 4. Distribution of the Residue
"SUBJECT to the trusts DECLARED above my Executors SHALL HOLD my Estate as follows:"
a) "Upon trust absolutely for my [relationship] [Name] if [he/she] shall survive me for the period of twenty eight days but if my said [relationship] shall die in my lifetime or shall not survive me for the period aforesaid or if this gift fails or lapses for any other reason and subject thereto"
b) "Upon trust in the following shares:"
  i) "[X]% to [beneficiary name] Provided that if my said [beneficiary] shall die without having attained a vested interest leaving issue who survive me then such issue shall take by substitution such failed share and if there shall be more than one of such issue they shall take in equal shares per stirpes but so that no issue shall take whose parent is alive and so capable of taking"
  [repeat for each beneficiary]
  
"If any of the share or shares under this sub clause b) shall fail in their entirety that share or those shares shall be added proportionally to the other shares that have not failed (and this provision shall apply to both an original share and an augmented share)"

### 5. Conditional Gift at Specified Age of 18 Years
"Any interest left in this my Will to a beneficiary shall be conditional on them attaining the age of 18 years and shall carry the intermediate interest until that age I give the power to my Executors in their absolute discretion to advance part or all of such entitlement which my Executors deem to be appropriate"

### 6. Executor and Trustee Powers
"My Executors and trustees shall in addition to and without prejudice to all statutory powers have the powers and immunities set out in The STEP Powers provided they shall not exercise any of their powers so as to conflict with the beneficial provisions of this my Will"

### 7. Survivorship
"Any Person who does not survive me by twenty eight days who would otherwise be a beneficiary under this my Will shall be treated for the purposes of my Will as having died in my lifetime"

### 8. Organ Donation (conditional — only if client opted in)
"I declare that it is my desire that after my death any of my organs can be used for therapeutic purposes"

### 9. Funeral Wishes
"I Desire that my body be [cremated/buried] and my ashes disposed of by my trustees and the expense thereof shall be a first charge on my Estate"

### 10. STEP Powers
"In this my Will where the context so admits any reference to the STEP Powers shall mean the Standard Provisions and all of the Special Provisions (with the exception of 18.2) of the Society of Trust and Estate Practitioners (3rd edition) shall apply"

### 11. For the Avoidance of Doubt
"I declare that"
a) "Clause headings are included for reference purposes only and do not affect the interpretation of this my Will"
b) "Words denoting the singular shall include the plural and vice versa and the masculine includes the feminine and vice versa"
c) "Person shall include a body of persons either corporate or incorporate"
d) "References to any statutory provision shall include any statutory modification or re-enactment of such provisions"
e) "Due to printing limitations the reverse side of every page of this my Will may be left blank"
f) "This is to be the last clause of my Will and shall be followed only by the attestation clause"

## Typography / Style
- Font: Garamond or similar classical serif
- Clause numbers: bold, e.g. "1. Revocation"
- Sub-items: a), b), i), ii) with indentation
- Borders: thin double-rule border on cover page
- Page numbers: "Page X of Y" centred at bottom
- Line spacing: 1.5–2x for readability

## Trust Clauses to Add (optional inserts after clause 4)
- **Protective Property Trust (PPT / Lifetime Trust)**: Inserted when client owns property jointly and wants to protect share for children. Clause creates a life interest trust over the property share for surviving spouse, remainder to children.
- **Discretionary Trust**: Inserted when client wants trustees to have full discretion over distribution of estate or a portion of it. Names trustees, beneficiaries class, and grants wide powers.
- **Vulnerable Person's Trust**: Inserted when a beneficiary has a disability or vulnerability. Qualifies for special tax treatment under Finance Act 2005. Names the vulnerable beneficiary and trustees.

## Mirror Will differences
- Two separate documents produced, one per client
- Each document mirrors the other (spouse/partner as primary beneficiary and executor)
- Clause 4a references the partner as primary beneficiary
- Clause 2 appoints the partner as primary executor
